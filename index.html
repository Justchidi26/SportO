About Us

Founded in 2020, EliteSport Limited is a duly registered sports management company committed to discovering, developing, and placing exceptional football talents across top clubs in Africa, Europe, America, and Asia.

Our team comprises experienced sports agents, journalists, coaches, professional scouts, and retired players who share a unified vision — bridging the gap between elite talent and world-class opportunities.

What We Do

Club Sourcing

Through our strong global network of scouts and football clubs, we identify and secure the right placement opportunities for our clients.

Negotiation

Our registered intermediaries manage contract negotiations with professionalism and integrity, ensuring favorable terms and long-term value.

Agent Representation

We provide holistic representation and personalized long-term career management tailored to each client's goals.

Public Relations

We manage media presence through press coverage, public appearances, and strategic brand positioning.

Projects

Our projects focus on continuous development by analyzing player strengths, improving weaknesses, and maximizing individual potential.
